GARY LEIGHTON

Guitar Teacher

With over 25 years of experience Gary brings knowledge, patience and dedication to the development of music enthusiasts of all ages. In an individual or group setting Gary will be able to help you reach your goals while making it fun. Whether you’re just starting out, looking to strengthen your talent to take it to the next level, or fine tuning for your own enjoyment, you’re in the right place.
Gary holds a Bachelor of Music (performance) Degree from The University of Massachusetts-Lowell and has lifelong experience in the music industry as a teacher, performer and booking agent. In 2005 Gary, along with Ralph Napolitano, opened Rockright Academy, a performance based rock school that specializes in group music lessons and summer rock camps.
For 20 years Gary performed throughout the Seacoast with the 80’s cover band Gazpacho. Prior to that, and currently, he plays guitar for an original Progressive Metal Band Divine Regale, which he formed with a few local musicians.
